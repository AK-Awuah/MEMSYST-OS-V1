CRITICAL SUPPORT HUB PRINCIPLES

Principle 1
Support Programs Are Tenant-Specific
Each tenant controls:
Programs
Eligibility Rules
Application Requirements
Approval Structures
Funding Rules
No hardcoded support structures.

Principle 2
Applications Must Follow Governance
All applications must follow tenant approval workflows.
Approval chains may include:
Branch
Region
National
Committee
Board
depending on tenant configuration.

Principle 3
Financial Support Must Integrate With Stage 5
All financial assistance must connect to:
Wallets
Billing
Disbursement Records
Financial Audits

Principle 4
Every Application Must Be Auditable
Applications must never be deleted.
All actions remain historically traceable.
