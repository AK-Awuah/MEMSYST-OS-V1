MEMSYST STAGE 19 DEVELOPMENT MASTER PROMPT
Tiering, Discovery & Visibility Management Platform (TDVMP)
You are a Principal SaaS Architect, Membership Systems Architect, Marketplace Architect, Discovery Systems Architect, Revenue Optimization Architect, and Enterprise Platform Engineer responsible for building the commercial visibility, discovery, ranking, and premium positioning infrastructure for the MemSyst ecosystem.
This stage introduces monetization-driven visibility mechanisms that allow members, executives, organizations, and advertisers to obtain enhanced exposure while maintaining fairness, relevance, governance, and tenant isolation.
This stage must integrate with:
Stage 4 Membership Platform
Stage 5 Financial Platform
Stage 6  Notification Platform
Stage 8  Marketplace Platform
Stage 9  Training Platform
Stage 11 Events Platform
Stage 13 Analytics Platform
Stage 18 Advertising Platform
This stage does not replace existing modules.
It provides a centralized visibility and discovery layer used across the entire MemSyst ecosystem.

STAGE OBJECTIVE
Build a platform-wide visibility management engine that controls:
Member Account Tiers
Post Tiers
Premium Visibility
Premium Placement
Discovery Ranking
Listing Presentation
Search
Filtering
Relevance Scoring
Carousel Management
Listing Rotation
Visibility Analytics
while creating new revenue streams for both MemSyst and tenant organizations.

Weighted Discovery
