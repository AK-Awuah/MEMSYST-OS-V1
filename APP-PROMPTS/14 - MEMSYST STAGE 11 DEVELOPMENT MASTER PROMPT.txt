CRITICAL EVENT MANAGEMENT PRINCIPLES

Principle 1
Events Are Tenant-Specific
Every event belongs to a tenant.
Examples:
GHABA Annual Congress
Regional Conference
Branch Meeting
Executive Retreat
Training Workshop
Events are isolated within their tenant environment.

Principle 2
Event Types Must Be Configurable
No hardcoded event structures.
Each tenant can create:
Conferences
Meetings
Workshops
Seminars
Trade Shows
Exhibitions
Award Ceremonies
Training Events
Custom Event Types

Principle 3
Attendance Must Be Verifiable
Attendance records must be:
Auditable
Trackable
Verifiable
and linked to:
Member Profiles
Credentials
Training Records
where applicable.

Principle 4
Event History Is Permanent
Events, registrations, and attendance records must never be deleted.
Historical participation forms part of member engagement history.
