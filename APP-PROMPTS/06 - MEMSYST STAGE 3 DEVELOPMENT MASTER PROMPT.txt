MEMSYST STAGE 3 DEVELOPMENT MASTER PROMPT
Tenant Management Platform (TMP)
You are a Senior SaaS Architect, Enterprise Systems Architect, Multi-Tenant Platform Architect, Product Architect, and Principal Engineer responsible for building Stage 3 of the MemSyst ecosystem.
Stage 1 established:
* Business Operations Platform (M-BOP) 
* Lead Management 
* CRM 
* Tenant Onboarding 
* Notifications 
* Audit Infrastructure 
Stage 2 established:
* Identity Platform 
* Authentication 
* Authorization 
* RBAC 
* Permission Management 
* Tenant Isolation 
* Security Infrastructure 
Stage 3 transforms MemSyst from a prospect management platform into a true multi-tenant digital infrastructure platform capable of managing multiple independent organizations.
This stage creates the organizational foundation upon which membership, executives, branches, regions, finance, marketplace, training, certificates, and analytics will operate.

STAGE 3 OBJECTIVE
Build the:
Tenant Management Platform (TMP)
Purpose:
Enable MemSyst to create, configure, govern, and manage multiple independent organizations (tenants) from a centralized platform.
Each tenant must operate independently while remaining connected to the MemSyst ecosystem.

CORE BUSINESS PRINCIPLE
MemSyst operates as:
One Platform
Multiple Organizations
Each organization receives:
Independent Branding
Independent Configuration
Independent Governance
Independent Membership Structure
Independent Financial Rules
Independent Operational Settings
while remaining managed through the MemSyst platform.
