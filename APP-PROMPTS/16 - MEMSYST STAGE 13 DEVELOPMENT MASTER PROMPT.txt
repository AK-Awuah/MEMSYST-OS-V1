CRITICAL ANALYTICS PRINCIPLES

Principle 1
Analytics Is Multi-Level
Analytics must support:
Platform Level
Tenant Level
National Level
Regional Level
Branch Level
Department Level
Program Level
Individual Level
depending on permissions.

Principle 2
Data Ownership Must Be Preserved
Tenant users may only access analytics for their tenant.
MemSyst administrators may access platform-wide analytics.

Principle 3
Reports Must Be Configurable
No hardcoded reporting assumptions.
Organizations must be able to create:
Custom Dashboards
Custom Reports
Custom KPIs
Custom Metrics

Principle 4
Analytics Must Be Real-Time Where Possible
Critical metrics should update automatically.
Historical reporting must remain available indefinitely.
