CRITICAL CREDENTIAL PRINCIPLES

Principle 1
Every Credential Must Be Verifiable
Every:
ID Card
Certificate
Credential
must contain a unique verification mechanism.
Recommended:
QR Code
Unique Verification Code
Verification URL
Verification must be publicly accessible.

Principle 2
Credentials Are Tenant-Specific
Each tenant controls:
Card Design
Certificate Design
Branding
Credential Rules
through configurable templates.

Principle 3
Credential History Must Be Permanent
No credential record should ever be deleted.
All lifecycle changes must remain auditable.

Principle 4
Credentials Are Lifecycle-Based
Credentials move through statuses rather than being recreated.
