CRITICAL PRINCIPLES

Principle 1
Human Governance Remains Supreme
AI may recommend.
AI may assist.
AI may automate.
AI must never override governance decisions without authorized approval.

Principle 2
AI Is Assistive, Not Authoritative
AI may:
Recommend
Analyze
Predict
Summarize
Automate
AI may not:
Approve Memberships
Approve Elections
Approve Financial Transactions
Override Governance Decisions
unless explicitly approved by authorized users.

Principle 3
Automation Must Be Configurable
Organizations must define:
Rules
Triggers
Conditions
Approvals
Actions
without modifying source code.

Principle 4
AI Must Respect Tenant Boundaries
Tenant data must never leak across organizations.
Every AI service must be tenant-aware.
