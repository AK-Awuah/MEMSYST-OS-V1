CRITICAL EXPERIENCE PRINCIPLES

Principle 1
Mobile First
All major platform functions must be accessible from mobile devices.
Members should not need desktop access for common tasks.

Principle 2
Role-Based Experiences
Users only see functionality relevant to their role.
Examples:
Member
Apprentice
Executive
Branch Officer
Regional Officer
Training Center
Tenant Administrator
Platform Administrator

Principle 3
Tenant Branding
Each tenant may customize:
Logo
Colors
Homepage
Menus
Terminology
Public Content
while operating on the shared MemSyst infrastructure.

Principle 4
Consistent Experience
Users should experience one platform regardless of module.
Navigation, notifications, identity, and workflows must remain consistent.
