CORE MEMBERSHIP PRINCIPLES
The following principles are mandatory.

Principle 1
One Person = One Account
A person may only have:
One Account
One Role
One Tenant
No duplicate membership accounts.

Principle 2
Members Register Apprentices
Apprentices cannot self-register.
Only an approved member can create an apprentice account.
Example:
Member
?
Registers Apprentice
?
Apprentice Linked To Member

Principle 3
Unlimited Apprentices
A member may register:
1 Apprentice
10 Apprentices
100 Apprentices
No platform limit.

Principle 4
Apprentices Belong To A Member
Every apprentice must be linked to:
Parent Member
at all times.

Principle 5
Apprentice Transfer
An apprentice may be transferred from:
Member A
?
Member B
with complete audit history.

Principle 6
Apprentice Upgrade
An apprentice may be upgraded to:
Master Member
Main Member
after satisfying tenant requirements.
Only authorized executives may approve upgrades.
