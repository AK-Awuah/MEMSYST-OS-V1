CRITICAL GOVERNANCE PRINCIPLES

Principle 1
Governance Is Tenant Controlled
Every tenant controls:
Governance Structure
Executive Positions
Election Rules
Voting Rules
Nomination Rules
Committee Structures
Approval Structures
No governance model should be hardcoded.

Principle 2
Only Eligible Members Can Participate
Voting rights must be determined through configurable eligibility rules.
Examples:
Active Membership
Membership Duration
Membership Category
Fee Compliance
Branch Membership
Executive Status

Principle 3
Every Vote Must Be Auditable
All voting activities must be:
Traceable
Auditable
Verifiable
Tamper Resistant
while supporting configurable anonymity rules.

Principle 4
Governance History Is Permanent
No governance records may be deleted.
Historical records become part of the organization's institutional memory.
