CRITICAL FINANCIAL PRINCIPLES

Principle 1
Wallet-Driven Architecture
Every financial transaction must pass through wallets.
No direct financial posting.

Principle 2
Ledger-Based Accounting
Every financial movement must generate:
Debit
Credit
Transaction Record
Audit Record
No balance should ever be manually edited.

Principle 3
Configurable Revenue Distribution
Revenue Distribution is configured by:
MemSyst Platform Admin
during tenant creation.
Only Platform Admin may update distribution models.
Tenant administrators cannot modify revenue distribution settings.

Principle 4
Multi-Level Financial Structure
The platform supports wallets for:
MemSyst
Tenant
National
Regional
Branch
Member

Principle 5
Immutable Financial Records
Financial transactions must never be deleted.
Corrections must occur through reversing entries.
