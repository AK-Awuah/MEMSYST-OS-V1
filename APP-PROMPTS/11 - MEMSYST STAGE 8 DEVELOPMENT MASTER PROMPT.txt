CRITICAL MARKETPLACE PRINCIPLES

Principle 1
Marketplace Is Tenant-Specific
Marketplace content belongs to the tenant where the member is registered.
Example:
GHABA Member
?
Creates Marketplace Post
?
Post Appears On GHABA Marketplace
Posts do not automatically appear across other tenant marketplaces.

Principle 2
Only Members Can Create Marketplace Posts
Based on the MemSyst business rules:
Only Members
may create marketplace listings.
Apprentices cannot create marketplace posts.

Principle 3
Marketplace Is Not E-Commerce
Stage 8 focuses on:
Promotion
Discovery
Lead Generation
Business Visibility
Not:
Shopping Cart
Order Fulfillment
Inventory Management
These may be future enhancements.

Principle 4
Compliance Monitoring
MemSyst Platform Administrators must be able to review marketplace content platform-wide.
This aligns with the original MemSyst architecture.
